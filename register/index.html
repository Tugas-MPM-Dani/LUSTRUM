What is creaticism

CREATICISM stands for Creative and Criticism, a national business case competition held by 180 Degrees Consulting Universitas Diponegoro for everyone in Indonesia which will take "Sustainable Business Practices: Innovating for the Better Future" as the theme. Not only a competition, but the event will be followed by webinar and international conference.

In CREATICISM, participants are called THE IDEATOR (Idea-Creator) who generate ideas and solutions for a given problem or challenge. These individuals use their creativity and imagination to come up with unique and innovative ideas that can help to solve a particular problem or improve a particular situation.

Terms and Conditions
